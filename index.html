est. 2017

DevAO was founded by Agustin Ortiz III in 2017. The name of the company derives from the passion for software development from Agustin. Hence the initials “AO” after what he loves to do, software DEVelopment. It first began with providing small local community clients with software solutions that resulted in increased efficiencies while solving problems. Since, DevAO has expanded throughout the midwest and the projects have become much more complex. Most recently, DevAO became a Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Company and was granted eligibility to do business with the US Federal Government. At DevAO we are dedicated to developing solutions to help businesses grow. Furthermore, we remain committed to advancing healthcare by providing people, software and collaborating with industry research/innovation.

Business App Stack

We can leverage each of our technology platforms into a single business application solution. The cloud can quickly build LAMP servers using AWS EC2 instances with a secure connection to an RDS MySQL database. The application framework can be built within an angular front-end and we can run all API calls through Wavemaker's RAD platform. This solution can quickly solve and secure a business data retrieval problem with concurrent users.

Managed Services

A package we offer to keep custom software stack maintained. We can manage clouds accounts (server maintenance), data services (clean up & reorg), and content management systems like Wordpress. The DevAO team expertise stretches across the entire DevOps stack.

ServiceNow - Reporting

As ServiceNow platform experts, we can stand up an instance of this IT Management System. After a migration and customization phase, SN powerful reporting tools allows users to pull custom reports for their business needs. This becomes a solution that doesn't require multiple phases of development and gets a business productivity to higher level.
